Who am I:
I (Hugo) am an aspiring film student. I have been editing since I was 13 years old. I've had a passion for film for a long time. Outside of film and editing I enjoy occasional reading, photography, fashion (by which I mean, scrolling on Pinterest and then going thrifting) and music. My school never had "film" as a subject to I've learnt everything I have through countless video essays. My dream is to one day be able to edit as a full-time career.
My History on YouTube:
In 2019 I uploaded my first actual YouTube video (no surprise that it was terrible). In June 2020, I created a whole new channel, now having learnt the basics of editing, and in a single day created my first viral video during lockdown "The Bee Movie but only when anyone says E" (inspired by GrayWalf) which currently has a total of 1.3m views and was edited in about 11 hours. In total, I made a bit over 100 low-quality meme videos, very few of which are still public now. The channel peaked at 5,010 subscribers and has gone down since due to a lack of uploads. The channel is (likely permanently) retired.
In June 2022 I made my third attempt at a gaming channel and created 15 videos over the course of a year. Editing processes for these videos ranged from 5hrs-50hrs (including my biggest project at the time, which, due to incomplete filming, remains unfinished). The channel is indefinitely retired (there is a fair chance I will try a few more videos at some point).
In Sept 2023 I started my first film-dedicated channel. My hope with this channel was to learn how to edit better and, was in general, just an excuse to edit, because of this, a lot of my videos will focus round a key editing technique. The channel remains open and active.
P.S. If I go inactive for an extended period of time, it is likely related to my schoolwork with the addition that I get motivation in spikes and dips.
Origins:
This unnamed little laddie was created one evening when I was complaining to Bread about how I was struggling with getting a good online profile picture and how I had been looking at tips and had numerous failed attempts. When I was complaining, Bread was making me a thumbnail and drew a little figure (which is still hidden in the thumbnail today) and when I said that I liked it, he copied it over and drew a full copy. The little lad now has many variations (including one wearing sunglasses) and is on my main 3 channels.
This name (Originating on 26th Nov) is based of a Mii name from my friend on the Wii entitled "Cibiwibi", however, after consideration, SneebyWeeby was chosen as it is easier to spell and read and sounds fairly silly, especially accompanied with the untitled little lad.
